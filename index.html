Biography

I am a PhD Candidate at NExT++, National University of Singapore, supervised by Prof. Tat-Seng Chua and mentored by Prof. Wang Xiang. With friends and collaborators, I develop models for video understanding and 3D scene understanding with stronger interpretability, generalization, and robustness.

My research is motivated by, and contributes to, multimodal learning across language, vision, video, and spatial signals.

I am always open to collaboration on video QA, 3D affordance learning, and multimodal reasoning. Please feel free to reach out via email if you are interested in working together.
