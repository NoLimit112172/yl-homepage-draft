About

Biography

I am a PhD Candidate at NExT++, National University of Singapore, supervised by Prof. Chua Tat-Seng and mentored by Prof. Wang Xiang. With friends and collaborators, I work on models for video and 3D scene understanding with better interpretability, generalization, and robustness. My research is motivated by, and contributes to, multimodal learning.

I am always open to collaboration. Please feel free to send me an email if you are interested.
