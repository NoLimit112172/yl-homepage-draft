个人简介Biography

李易聪现任 中国科学技术大学 电子工程与信息科学系（六系）教师， 主持多模态多智能体实验室， 实验室位于高新校区信智楼 B906。

Yicong Li is a faculty member at the Department of Electronic Engineering and Information Science (EEIS), University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). He leads the Multimodal Multi-Agent Laboratory, located at Room B906, Information & Intelligence Building, High-tech Campus.

课题组面向多模态感知与理解、多智能体协同与智能决策等方向开展研究， 探索语言、视觉、视频等多源信息融合下的可解释、可泛化智能系统， 并推动相关技术在真实场景中的应用。

Our group works on multimodal perception and understanding, multi-agent collaboration, and intelligent decision-making, building interpretable and generalizable systems that fuse language, vision, video, and spatial signals for real-world applications.

欢迎对多模态学习、多智能体系统及相关方向感兴趣的同学与研究者联系合作。 请通过邮件 liyicong@ustc.edu.cn 与我取得联系。
I welcome collaborations on multimodal learning, multi-agent systems, and related topics. Please feel free to reach out via liyicong@ustc.edu.cn.
